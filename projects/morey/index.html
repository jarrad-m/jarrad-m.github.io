Welcome to Morey Security

Originally established as Morey Electrics Company in 1972, as an Electrical Contracting business.

In 1985 the market indicated a change was required, and the business redirected its focus on to the specialised services fields of security and communications, and subsequently access control and CCTV. In 1988 the business was incorporated as Morey Electronic Security Pty Ltd.

The company has varied in size with staff levels from 2 to 18 over the years depending on market requirements. The company had trained eight apprentices during the earlier years, one of whom had a second placing in the Apprentice of the Year Awards. Several of our employees subsequently became Contractors in their own right

Morey Security prides itself on good customer relations and excellent work practices, and due to our good relationship with our customers gains a large amount of referals for new works.
